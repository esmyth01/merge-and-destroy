About Me

I grew up in central Michigan. I graduated from the University of Michigan in 2008 with a BA. I enjoy many different activities but the ones I love most usually involve the outdoors. In particular I love to fly fish and downhill ski. I moved to Seattle in 2008, and absolutely love it out here. The people, the culture, and outdoor lifestyle coincide perfectly with my personality.

Why I'm Taking This Class

I moved out to Seattle shortly after graduating from UofM in 2008. Fresh out of college with a new degree in hand, I figured it would be no problem finding a good salary job. Jobs were few and pay was minimal, and after bouncing around in many different fields of work, I said enough is enough and decided to go back to school. After many sleepless nights spent researching and second-guessing myself, I elected to give the web development program a try at SCCC. I'm really excited about what it offers. I'm hoping that upon completion, I will be able to land a good job with a good salary. My fingers are crossed.
