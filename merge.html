Why I'm Taking This Class

I moved out to Seattle shortly after graduating from UofM in 2008. Fresh out of college with a new degree in hand, I figured it would be no problem finding a good salary job. As it turns out, I may have been a little naive. Jobs were few and pay was minimal, and after bouncing around in many different fields of work, I said enough is enough and decided to go back to school. After many sleepless nights spent researching and second-guessing myself, I elected to give the web development program a try at SCCC. I'm hoping that upon completion, I will be able to land a good job with a good salary. My fingers are crossed.
